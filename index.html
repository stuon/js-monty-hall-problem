Monty Hall Problem

Welcome to the Monty Hall Problem game. Behind one of the doors is a car. Behind the other two are goats.

1. What are the odds of picking the door that has the car?

2. After your first selection, you will be shown one of the other door that has a goat, and given the choice to switch to the remaining door. If you always swtich, what are the odds of picking the door that has the car?
